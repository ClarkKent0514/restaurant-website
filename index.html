About

At El Poco Calavera, we serve beverages, takeout snacks, and small meals; quesadillas, chilaquiles, or fajitas being a few of our items. We proudly serve traditional dishes that people are sure to enjoy, whether it be just a small snack to go, or a more sizable meal for a lunch, we’ve got many of our own Mexican dishes to serve for you!

Specialties

Our specialties include small snacks and pastry-like foods, like conchas, taquitos, and even pan de muerto on Día de los Muertos! We are also happy to provide drinks such as aqua fresca and others of your choice. We’ll gladly serve you anything of your liking, whether it be chile verde or simple menudo.

Type of Food

The type of food we serve is traditional Mexican-Style food, whether it be for a special holiday, or just a simple outing, we have the dish your stomach is craving!
